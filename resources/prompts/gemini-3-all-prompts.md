## 1. Logo — Primary Wordmark

> **Note:** An SVG version already exists at `resources/logo/ai-forte-logo.svg`. Use this Gemini prompt only if you want a raster/photorealistic alternative.

```
Create a minimalist, premium tech brand logo for "AI Forte" — an AI orchestrator
agency. The logo should be a horizontal wordmark lockup: a geometric icon on the
left, wordmark "AI Forte" on the right.

Icon: an abstract representation of orchestration — three or four interconnected
nodes forming a subtle network pattern, or a stylized geometric shape suggesting
coordination and flow. Think: modern, confident, not cartoonish, not generic.

Typography: "AI Forte" in a modern geometric sans-serif, high contrast,
slightly condensed. "AI" should be subtly emphasized (bolder weight or accent color).

Color palette: deep midnight navy (#0A1628) for the wordmark, accent in
electric indigo (#5B5BFF) or vibrant coral (#FF5B5B) for the icon and "AI".
Clean white background.

Style: flat vector, crisp edges, premium tech brand feel, reminiscent of Stripe,
Linear, or Vercel branding. No gradients, no 3D, no shadows.

Render at high resolution, centered composition, generous whitespace.
Return as a clean logo asset suitable for a website header and business card.
```

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific about composition** — left/right percentages, top/bottom regions
2. **Name exact hex colors** — Gemini 3 respects hex codes well
3. **Reference brand archetypes** — "like Stripe, Linear, Vercel" converges faster than abstract descriptions
4. **Say what you DON'T want** — "no 3D," "no generic AI brain imagery," "no gradients"
5. **Iterate conversationally** — after first generation, reply with refinements: "make the icon 20% smaller," "shift accent to more coral," "add more whitespace"
6. **Use Nano Banana Pro model** — best quality, supports up to 14 reference images
7. **Generate 4 variations** per prompt — "give me 4 variations of this concept"
8. **For brand consistency** — after generating the logo, upload it as a reference image for subsequent prompts and say "use this logo and color palette"
