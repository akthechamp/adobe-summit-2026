## Universal exam rules (applies to all 4)

- **Pacing: 60-65 sec/question.** Day 1 rushing cost you 15-20 points.
- **Two-pass strategy:** Confident answers first, mark-and-return for hard ones.
- **Eliminate distractors:** Adobe MCQs usually have 1 obvious wrong, 1 right, 2 nearly-right. Kill the obvious first.
- **Read each question twice before looking at options.** Disambiguators hide in clauses.
- **Watch keywords:** "BEST", "MOST", "PRIMARY" = pick closest right; "ALWAYS", "NEVER" = usually wrong.
- **Flag-and-return** when unsure; don't spend >3 min on any question.
- **Trust your Magento/HubSpot background** — you know more than you think.
