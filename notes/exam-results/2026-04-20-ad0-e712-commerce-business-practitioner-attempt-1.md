## What this cert actually is

**AD0-E712 is the Business Practitioner track** — not the Commerce Developer exam. It's aimed at:
- Merchandisers, catalog managers
- Digital marketers running Commerce storefronts
- Business analysts + project managers who spec Commerce features
- Agency account leads pitching Commerce implementations

It's a conceptual exam covering how Adobe Commerce / Magento Open Source works at the *user* + *decision-maker* level — catalog structure, cart rules, price rules, customer groups, GA integrations, SEO basics, PCI-DSS awareness, GDPR basics. It does **not** test PHP, XML, plugins, CLI commands, or code-level work.
